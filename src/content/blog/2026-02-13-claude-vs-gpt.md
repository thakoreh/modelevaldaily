## Claude vs GPT: The Big Question

Two companies dominate AI: Anthropic (Claude) and OpenAI (GPT). Here's the definitive comparison.

## Quick Answer

- **For coding:** Claude 4
- **For agents:** GPT-5
- **For writing:** GPT-5
- **For reasoning:** Tie
- **For value:** GPT-5

## Detailed Comparison

### Coding

**Claude 4:** Produces cleaner, more maintainable code. Better at understanding existing codebases. Superior debugging.

**GPT-5:** Slightly less polished but faster. Better for quick prototypes.

**Winner:** Claude 4

### Reasoning

**Claude 4:** Excellent step-by-step reasoning. Good at math and logic.

**GPT-5:** Strong reasoning with good chain-of-thought. Slightly better at complex multi-step problems.

**Winner:** Tie

### Writing

**Claude 4:** Good but sometimes overly cautious.

**GPT-5:** More engaging, natural writing style. Better for content creation.

**Winner:** GPT-5

### Agents

**Claude 4:** Good tool use but slower.

**GPT-5:** Best-in-class for autonomous agents. Fast, great function calling.

**Winner:** GPT-5

### Cost

| Model | Input | Output |
|-------|-------|--------|
| Claude 4 | $15.00 | $75.00 |
| GPT-5 | $15.00 | $60.00 |

GPT-5 is 20% cheaper on output.

## Final Verdict

Use Claude 4 for code quality. Use GPT-5 for everything else.
